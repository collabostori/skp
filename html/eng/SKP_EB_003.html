Cloud Streaming

Virtualization-based media solutions
Cloud Streaming is a screen virtualization technology-based media solution that executes all computing in the cloud. Terminals such as set-top boxes transmit screens processed in the cloud and deliver it to users. Unlike previous methods of direct computing on terminals, cloud streaming computes on high performance servers, improving on service quality and also significantly reducing latency. Media businesses can establish an innovative media platform that supports various services including high-performance and high-quality UI, T-Commerce, and advertising via Cloud Streaming solutions, through which they can provide high-quality services to their customers.
