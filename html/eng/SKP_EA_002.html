Company OVERVIEW

SK플래닛은 변화와 혁신으로 세계 시장을 선도하는 ICT 전문 기업입니다.

SSK Planet has a wide range of expertise in data infrastructure-based ICT synergy, marketing platform, and data business.

We provide differentiated customer values with our concentrated data and solution expertise and lead the development

capability of our SK ICT Family. We strive to be Korea’s top data &tech company with our customer and market insight,

technology and service innovation, and forward-thinking challenging spirit.
