RubixBrain

Sophisticated IoT based sensor and data analysis achieved through deep learning
technology
Tapping into cloud-based deep learning and real-time high-volume data analysis technology, SK Planet automatically monitors sensor error occurrence and provides various data analysis and its visualization services based on SK Planet’s 20 billion incidences of accumulated sensor data (for example, fine dust, integrated air quality, flood detection, vibration, etc.). This enables customers to increase sensor observation accuracy and improve data-based decision-making reliability and implement advanced yet affordable sensor management solutions.

Weatherpong

Most accurate local weather notifying weather information application
Weatherpong is an application that provides real-time weather information based on the user's current location or from a 1 to 2 km radius of an area of interest. It shows per-minute real-time information such as temperature, rainfall, rain probability, wind speed, cloud distribution, and humidity, by integrating the only domestic tightly-knitted weather observation network to have more than 1,000 sensors in the metropolitan area and the information from the Meteorological Agency. ‘Pong’, the character that expresses emotions according to the weather, delivers weather information in a more exciting and differentiated way. Moreover, it provides detailed weather conditions such as fine dust, ultraviolet rays, sunset, sunrise, hourly and daily forecasts, and more. In the event of special weather reports such as typhoons, heightened fine dust levels, etc., it alerts with pop-ups, providing customer convenience. Weatherpong is available for download on One Store, Google Play, and the Apple App Store regardless of your mobile carrier.
